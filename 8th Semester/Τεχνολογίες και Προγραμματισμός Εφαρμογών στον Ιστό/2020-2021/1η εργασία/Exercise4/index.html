Introduction

In this article we are going to take a look at the cyber attack that hit SolarWinds and every major corporation, organisation and government agency that it affected.
The attack became known at 13th of December, 2020, although it has been latent and active for months before that date.
The attackers exploited a vulnerability in one of SolarWind's system in order to isnert their own malicious code.
The same code was rolled out as part of routine software updates the company sent to its clients, which in turn made each and every one of them vulnerable to the same exploit.
The impact of the attack was not only financial, as major concerns were raised as to which systems have been compromised and what kind of data was leaked.

What is SolarWinds?

SolarWinds is the name of a major IT software company.
They provide softare for Network, System as well as Database Management, being more well-known for the former of the three.
The company was founded in 1999 by the brothers Donald and David Yonce. Its customer base includes many U.S. agencies as well more than 400 companies in the Fortune 500 list. The most famous of SolarWinds' products is Orion.

What is a supply chain attack?

A supply chain attack aims to gain access to a system by exploiting a vulnerability in third-party software that the system uses.
Every system is a collection of communicating sub-systems. By finding a weak link in this chain of dependencies an attacker can potentially infiltrate all other systems that depend on it.
